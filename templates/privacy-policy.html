Welcome to Jobcus (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We’re committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile Data: Name, email, password, and any profile details you provide when signing up.
  • Resume & Document Uploads: Files (PDF, DOCX) you upload for analysis and optimisation.
  • Chat & Interaction Logs: Messages you send to our AI chat coach, interview answers, and feedback responses.
  • Usage & Analytics: Pages you visit, features you use, device/browser details (via cookies and analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To analyze and score your resume, generate AI-powered suggestions, and simulate interview Q&As.
  • Personalization: To tailor content-like job insights and coaching tips-based on your career goals.
  • Communication: To send you account notifications, product updates, and marketing (with your consent).
  • Improvement & Research: To aggregate usage data for improving our AI models and user experience.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and email-delivery partners under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate safeguards.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or export the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Authenticate your sessions.
  • Remember your preferences.
  • Gather analytics about feature usage.

You can control cookies via your browser settings; disabling them may affect the site’s functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, secure servers) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep them confidential.

7. Children’s Privacy

Jobcus is intended for users 13 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We’ll post the date of last revision at the top. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please email us at support@jobcus.com.
