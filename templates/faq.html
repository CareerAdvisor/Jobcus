❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jobcus? +
Jobcus is an AI-powered career assistant designed to help job seekers build optimized resumes, analyze their skill gaps, prepare for interviews, and gain job market insights — all in one platform.
Is Jobcus free to use? +
Jobcus offers both free and premium features. You can build and analyze resumes for free, while advanced features may require a subscription.
How does the Resume Builder work? +
Simply fill in your career details and our AI formats it into a professionally structured resume. You can then download your resume in PDF, Word, or TXT formats.
Can I download resumes in different formats? +
Yes, Jobcus allows you to export resumes in PDF, Word, and plain text formats.
What is the Skill Gap Analysis tool? +
It analyzes your current resume and career goals, then highlights missing skills and suggests how to acquire them.
How secure is my data on Jobcus? +
We use industry-standard encryption and follow best practices to keep your data safe. We never share your information without your consent.
Do I need to create an account? +
No account is required to generate a resume or use some features. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress and access premium tools.
How does the AI Interview Coach help me? +
Our AI Interview Coach helps you rehearse job interview questions, suggests best answers, and provides feedback to boost your confidence.
