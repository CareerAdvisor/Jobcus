Frequently Asked Questions

Find your answers here.

01/ What is Jobcus?
Jobcus is an AI-powered career assistant designed to help job seekers build optimized resumes, analyze their skill gaps, prepare for interviews, and gain job market insights — all in one platform.
02/ Is Jobcus free to use?
Yes, Jobcus offers core features for free. Premium features and tools may require a subscription.
03/ How does the Resume Builder work?
You fill in your career details and our AI instantly formats it into a professional resume ready for download and optimization.
04/ Can I download resumes in different formats?
Yes. Resumes can be downloaded in DOCX, PDF, and TXT formats to suit your needs.
05/ What is the Skill Gap Analysis tool?
This tool helps you identify the skills you’re missing for a specific career goal and suggests ways to improve.
06/ How secure is my data on Jobcus?
We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage to protect your personal information and uploaded resumes.
07/ Do I need to create an account?
No account is required to explore the platform, but saving your progress, downloads, or accessing premium features may require sign-up.
08/ How does the AI Interview Coach help me?
Our AI Interview Coach helps you rehearse job interview questions, suggests best answers, and provides feedback to boost your confidence.
09/ Can I upload my existing resume?
Yes! You can upload a PDF version of your resume, and our AI will analyze and suggest improvements instantly.
10/ I found a bug or have feedback. What should I do?
We’d love to hear from you! Use our contact form or reach out via support@jobcus.com. Your feedback helps us improve.
