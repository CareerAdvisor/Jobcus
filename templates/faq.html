What is Jobcus?

Jobcus is an AI-powered career assistant that helps you build resumes, analyze job market data, identify skill gaps, and prepare for interviews.

Is Jobcus free to use?

Yes, Jobcus offers core features for free. Premium features and tools may require a subscription.

How does the Resume Builder work?

You fill in your career details and our AI instantly formats it into a professional resume ready for download and optimization.

Can I download resumes in different formats?

Yes. Resumes can be downloaded in DOCX, PDF, or TXT format.

What is the Skill Gap Analysis tool?

This tool compares your resume with target job requirements to identify skills you may need to acquire to become more competitive.

How secure is my data on Jobcus?

We use secure, encrypted protocols and never store resumes without your consent.

Do I need to create an account?

Jobcus works without login for most tools. Account creation will unlock saved history and resume versioning features soon.

How does the AI Interview Coach help me?

It simulates real-world interview scenarios, gives tips, and helps you build confidence with feedback.
