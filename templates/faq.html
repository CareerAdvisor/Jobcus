❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to making the most of Jobcus – your AI Career Advisor

🔍 What is Jobcus?

Jobcus is an AI-powered career assistant designed to help job seekers build optimized resumes, analyze their skill gaps, prepare for interviews, and gain job market insights — all in one platform.

📄 How does the Resume Builder work?

Simply fill in your career details and our AI formats it into a professionally structured resume. You can then download your resume in PDF, Word, or TXT formats.

⚙️ What is Resume Optimization?

Our AI scans your resume and offers an optimized version tailored for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) to improve your chances of getting hired.

🎯 What is the Resume Analyzer?

The analyzer scores your resume based on keywords, relevance, and formatting. It also highlights areas you can improve for better results.

📊 What kind of insights can I get from Job Insights?

You can discover average salaries, job trends, in-demand skills, and opportunities by region — helping you make informed career choices.

💼 What is the Interview Coach?

Our AI Interview Coach helps you rehearse job interview questions, suggests best answers, and provides feedback to boost your confidence.

🤖 How is Jobcus different from other career tools?

Jobcus combines AI resume writing, real-time job market data, personalized interview coaching, and skill gap analysis — all in one simple platform.

🌍 Is Jobcus free to use?

Jobcus offers both free and premium features. You can build and analyze resumes for free, while advanced features may require a subscription.

📥 Can I upload my existing resume?

Yes! You can upload a PDF version of your resume, and our AI will analyze and suggest improvements instantly.

🛠️ I found a bug or have feedback. What should I do?

We’d love to hear from you! Use our contact form or reach out via support@jobcus.com. Your feedback helps us improve.
